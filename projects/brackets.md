# Poisson and Jacobi Brackets

In this code, we have examined the Poisson and Jacobi brackets. First, we use Poisson bracket to solve the EOM for the harmonic oscillator. Then, we verified several properties of the Poisson and Jacobi bracket such as skew-symmetry and Jacobi identity.
